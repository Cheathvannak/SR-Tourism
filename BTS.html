Banteay Srei

Geography

Banteay Srei (Lady Temple) is located about 30km from the Siem Reap center. It is accessible by almost every kinds of transpotations you can think of.

History

This temple is thought to be rediscovered back in the 13th or 14th century due to its beautiful carvings. It is the only major temple not to be built by a king.

Architecture

This temple is known for its Architecture. The Hindu structure is covered in carvings of beautiful Apsaras as well as many other things.
