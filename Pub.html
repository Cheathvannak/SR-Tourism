PUB STREET

History

Pub Street originated from a 3-restaurant establishment with its first club called 'Angkor WHAT?' opening in 1998.

What Is Pub Street Known For?

Pub Street or 'Street 8' is known for its variety of places ranging from clubs to fish massages. It is popular for its nightlife, you simply can't get the experience from anywhere else in the world.
